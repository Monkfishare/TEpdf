MGR

Nickname: Revolutionary leader

Hero of 136 films, always played good

guy who never smoked or drank on screen

Power Chief minister, Tamil Nadu

Karunanidhi

Nickname: Scholar of arts

Scriptwriter of 61 films, most of which

propagated Dravidian ideas

Power Chief minister, Tamil Nadu

Nickname: Mother

Acted in 140 films, including dozens

as MGR’s love interest

Power Chief minister,

Tamil Nadu

Nickname: Respected leader

smoking cigarettes with panache
