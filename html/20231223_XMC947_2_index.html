How hydrogen can be formed

How hydrogen can be extracted

Radiolysis

From traps

Energy from radioactive rocks splits

water into hydrogen and oxygen

Drilling into hydrogen trapped below

impermeable rock or salt layers

Serpentinisation

Direct

Iron-rich rocks react with very hot water

to produce iron oxide and hydrogen

Harvesting hydrogen directly from

fractured rocks rich in iron

Deep-seated formation

Enhanced

Hydrogen is produced deep in

the Earth’s core or mantle

Stimulating hydrogen production by

pumping water into iron-rich rocks
